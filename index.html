Forecasting Brent Crude Oil Prices using Xgboost.

The purpose of this project is to develop a machine learning model that can accurately forecast the Brent crude oil daily prices using Xgboost and Sci-Kit Learn. Brent crude oil is a benchmark used to price two-thirds of the world's oil, including oils exported from the North Sea, Europe and West Africa. Understanding and accurately forecasting the price of Brent crude oil is crucial for oil-producing countries and organizations in the oil industry.
